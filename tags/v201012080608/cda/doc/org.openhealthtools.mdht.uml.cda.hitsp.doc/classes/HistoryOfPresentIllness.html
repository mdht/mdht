History Of Present Illness

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.3.88.11.83.107]

The History of Present Illness Section contains information about the sequence of events preceding the patient's current complaints.

  1. Conforms to IHE History Of Present Illness template (templateId: 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.3.4)

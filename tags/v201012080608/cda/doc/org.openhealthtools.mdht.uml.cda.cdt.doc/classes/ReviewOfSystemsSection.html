Review Of Systems Section

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.4.10]

The review of systems is a relevant collection of symptoms and functions systematically gathered by a clinician. It includes symptoms the patient is currently experiencing, some of which were not elicited during the history of present illness, as well as a potentially large number of pertinent negatives, e.g., symptoms that the patient was specifically asked if they had experienced or were currently experiencing, but had denied experiencing.

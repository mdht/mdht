Chief Complaint Section Proc Note

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.18.2.16]

The Chief Complaint section records the patient's chief complaint (the patient's own description). The Chief Complaint section may be a subsection of the Medical History section.

  1. SHALL conform to CDA Section
  2. SHALL contain [1..1] code/@code = "10154-3" CHIEF COMPLAINT (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1 LOINC STATIC) (CONF-PN-108)
  3. MAY satisfy: If the Chief Complaint section is NOT present, there MAY be a statement in the Medical History section providing the patient's chief compliant. (CONF-PN-109)

Medications Section

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.3.88.11.83.112]

The Medications Section contains information about the relevant medications for the patient. At a minimum, the currently active medications should be listed.

  1. Conforms to IHE Medications Section template (templateId: 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.3.19)
  2. C83-[CT-112-2]: SHALL contain [1..*] entry, such that it
    1. contains Medication (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.3.88.11.83.8)

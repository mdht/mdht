Plan Of Care Activity Supply

[Supply: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.1.25]

  1. Conforms to CDA Supply
  2. Conforms to Plan Of Care Activity
  3. SHALL contain [1..1] @moodCode
  4. SHALL contain [1..*] id
  5. SHALL satisfy: moodCodeValue
    • [OCL]: self.moodCode = vocab::x_DocumentSubstanceMood::INT or self.moodCode = vocab::x_DocumentSubstanceMood::RQO
  or self.moodCode = vocab::x_DocumentSubstanceMood::PRMS or self.moodCode = vocab::x_DocumentSubstanceMood::PRP

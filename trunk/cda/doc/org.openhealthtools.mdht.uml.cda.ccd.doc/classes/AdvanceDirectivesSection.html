Advance Directives Section

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.1.1]

  1. Conforms to CDA Section
  2. SHALL contain [1..1] code/@code = "42348-3" Advance directives (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1 LOINC STATIC 2.26)
  3. SHALL contain [1..1] title
  4. SHALL contain [1..*] entry, such that it
    1. contains Advance Directive Observation (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.1.17)

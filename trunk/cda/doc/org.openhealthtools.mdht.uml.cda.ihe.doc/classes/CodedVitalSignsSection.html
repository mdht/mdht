Coded Vital Signs Section

[Section: templateId 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.1.5.3.2]

The vital signs section contains coded measurement results of a patient's vital signs.

  1. Conforms to Vital Signs Section template (templateId: 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.3.25)
  2. SHALL contain [1..*] entry, such that it
    1. contains Vital Signs Organizer (templateId: 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.4.13.1)
